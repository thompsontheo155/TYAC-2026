Community Impact Goals

Community Impact Goals (CIG) are clear objectives aimed at creating positive lasting change in a community.

🕊️

Peacebuilding and Conflict Resolution

Focus on resolving disputes fostering understanding, and creating long-term harmony within communities.
They involve dialogue, empathy, and cooperation to address root causes of conflict and prevent future tensions.

🧠

Mental Health and Well-being

Mental health and Well-being are essential for living a balanced, fulfilling life.
They involve our emotional, and social wellness - how we think, feel and cope with stress.

Our Mission & Vision

To cultivate an inclusive ecosystem where young individuals are equipped with the skills, structural support, and leadership frameworks necessary to orchestrate impactful community adjustments.

Our History

From deep-rooted community volunteer actions to structured provincial operations, TYAC continues to deploy essential solutions—such as our flagship school initiatives and regional environmental protection sanitation exercises and others essential issues effecting young people livilihood. The Youth Alliance For Change was founded on August 4, 2025 to make a difference.
